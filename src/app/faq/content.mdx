# Balance

Stats are based loosely around chess elo.  Each 5 points is 400 elo, meaning rolling at your level is a 50/50 chance, and one level below is a 90/10 chance, and vice versa for above.
x   - clueless
0   - shitter
400 - noob
800 - beginner
1200 - intermediate
1600 - advanced
2000 - expert
2400 - pro
2800 - best ever
3200 - superhuman
3600 - perfect
4000 - transcendent
